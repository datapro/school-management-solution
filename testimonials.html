Assemblies of God High School is a co-educational privately- owned secondary school (Day & Boarding). A.G.H.S was established to provide qualitative education coupled with raising a generation of spiritually and morally sound learners. We register students for SSCE, NECO (Senior & Junior BECE) and Ogun State examination
