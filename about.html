About AGHS

A word from the Proprietor

It is a great delight indeed for me to welcome you all to Assemblies of God High School, Mowe, Ogun State. Historically, AGHS was established in September, 2004 by Retired Rev. I.M. Mpamaugo. The establishment of the school was necessitated by the need to raise life champions, who apart from becoming academically distinguished, physically and socially distinguished, would become morally and spiritually assertive, and shine as leading lights in their generations. Realistically, the accomplishment would ever had remained an apparition without the provision of effective and effecient leadership. Thus, the leadership of the Assemblies Of God High School, Lagos Mainland District, which currently is saddled with the management of the institution, is working relentlessly to provide necessary infrastructural facilities, and offer the required motivation to our workforce to enable us achieve the desired outcomes. This is why our teachers are dedicated and committed to nursing our students into godly maturity. The Guidance and Counselling unit of the school is functional, as the moral upbringing of our students is of paramount importance to us. Obviously, the current realities across different climes, especially, Nigeria, call for urgent need to inculcate the much needed moral values in our children. If all you desire is a school with a unique passion for the development of the total child, I am glad to recommend AGHS to you.

Rev. E. O. Awazie, PhD
District Superintendent,
Lagos Mainland District
Assemblies of God Nigeria,
